Music NFTs for an emerging genre and new generation of global performing artists.

Under the Nigerian and African genre, Afrobeat Music has become mainstream around the world and are now a must play in European and American night clubs and radio channels.

Genres such as hiplife, jùjú music, highlife and naija beats, which were amalgamated under the 'afrobeats' umbrella now stream on several international streaming platforms like Spotify, iTunes, Amazon music, gbedu.live, Soundcloud and more.

Afrobeats collaborations are becoming a must have for global Hip-hop and RnB artists like Drake and WizKid, 2021 Grammy award winner Burna boy and Chris Martins, Chris Brown and Davido to mention a few.

NFTs for the Nollywood movie industry.

Nifty Row seeks to deliver a unique platform that will enable creators like animators and producers issue and auction NFTs gloabally.

The marketplace will leverage on the global success of Nollywood films which currently airs across Africa, the US, UK, Europe and other parts of the world.

Investors of motion picture rights can be rest assured of incomes as the movies air over multiple cable and satellite networks such as Sky TV, DSTV, Netflix, Iroko TV, Amazon prime etc.
